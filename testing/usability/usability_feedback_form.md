# Usability Feedback Form — SRH Education Platform

**Substitute disclosure:** this structured feedback form is the **lighter-weight
substitute** for a live moderated session (per the assignment's allowance). It is
ready to administer to 3–5 testers; when run, transcribe each tester's numbers into
`usability/results_raw.csv` and summarise in `usability/RESULTS.md`.

**App under test:** https://srh-frontend.vercel.app
**Defined task (give verbatim):** *"Using this app, find out how to get tested for an
STI confidentially. Start from the front page."*

## Open questions
1. What was the single most confusing moment?
2. What would you change first?
3. Anything that made you trust — or distrust — the answer?

## Facilitator scoring
- **Success rate** = testers who completed unassisted ÷ total.
- **Mean time-to-task**, **mean missteps**, **mean of Q1–Q5** (report per language if mixed).
- Flag any statement whose mean < 3.0 as an action item.
